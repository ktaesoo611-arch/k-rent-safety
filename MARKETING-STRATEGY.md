## 1. Target Audience Profile

### Primary Audience: Foreign Renters in Seoul

**Demographics**:
- Age: 22-45
- Occupation: Students, professionals, digital nomads, English teachers
- Income: Mid to high (can afford jeonse deposits)
- Language: English-speaking, limited Korean proficiency
- Location: Currently in Seoul or planning to move within 3-6 months

**Pain Points**:
- Cannot read Korean real estate documents
- Fear of jeonse fraud (frequent news coverage)
- Don't know how to verify property safety
- Limited access to property valuation data
- Distrust of landlords and real estate agents
- Lack of legal knowledge about Korean rental laws

**Motivations**:
- Protect large deposit amounts (‚Ç©10M - ‚Ç©500M+)
- Avoid rental scams and financial loss
- Understand property risks before signing

## 11. Risk Mitigation

### Potential Challenges

**Challenge 1**: Platform spam restrictions
- **Solution**: Follow 90/10 rule (90% value, 10% promotion)

**Challenge 2**: Low conversion from awareness to signup
- **Solution**: Improve landing page, add social proof, simplify signup

**Challenge 3**: Users don't complete analysis
- **Solution**: Email nurture, simplified process, progress indicators

**Challenge 4**: Negative reviews from bad experiences
- **Solution**: Proactive support, clear disclaimers, quality assurance

**Challenge 5**: Competitors copying the model
- **Solution**: Build strong brand, community, partnerships
