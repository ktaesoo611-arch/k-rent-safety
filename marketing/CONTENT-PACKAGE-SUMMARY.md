## üí° Content Usage Tips

### Blog Posts

**SEO Optimization:**
- Submit all URLs to Google Search Console
- Build internal links between posts
- Target one primary keyword per post
- Include meta descriptions (provided in each file)
- Add images/infographics (create separately)

**Distribution:**
- Share on all social media channels
- Post to relevant subreddits (r/Living_in_Seoul, r/korea)
- Share in Facebook expat groups
- Email to your user list
- Repurpose into shorter social media posts

**Updating:**
- Review quarterly for accuracy
- Update statistics annually
- Add new case studies as they occur
- Update pricing when beta ends

### Social Media Posts

**Best Practices:**
- Post during peak times (7-9 PM KST weekdays, 10 AM-12 PM weekends)
- Engage with all comments within 2 hours
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags on Instagram
- Pin top-performing posts
- Save high-engagement posts to re-share later

**Adaptation:**
- Shorten for Twitter (280 characters)
- Add images for Instagram
- Combine 2-3 points into carousel posts
- Film video versions for TikTok
- Create poll versions for engagement

**Tracking:**
- Note which posts get most engagement
- Double down on successful formats
- Test different times/days
- Track conversion (clicks to website)

### Partnership Emails

**Personalization Required:**
- Replace ALL [bracketed] sections
- Research recipient before sending
- Reference specific company details
- Adjust formality to match recipient
- Include relevant case studies

**Sending Strategy:**
- Send 10 emails per day max (avoid spam)
- Space sends throughout week
- Follow up after 1 week if no response
- Track responses in spreadsheet
- A/B test subject lines

**Success Metrics:**
- Open rate: Track via email platform
- Response rate: Aim for 15-20%
- Meeting booking rate: Aim for 50% of responders
- Deal close rate: Aim for 30% of meetings
